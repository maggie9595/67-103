"I'll be Bach in a minuet..."

Steven Curtis Chapman - This Moment

One might think that 20 years would wear a person down, exhaust their creativity, and diminish their resolve. Not so. In This Moment, Steven Curtis Chapman is as infectiously energetic, artistically creative, and steady as ever. In fact, This Moment breaks new ground and demonstrates the continuing cutting-edge development of a phenomenal artist who has chosen not to focus on himself but on the God who gives such gifts. You just can't put SCC in a box -- he simply can't be defined easily.

It's the same SCC but different-new songs, new sounds, and fresh energy. I believe there to be more potential hits on this album than anything we've seen before from SCC. Cinderella, Yours, Children of God, With One Voice are all potential chart-toppers. Cinderella is a great song and a potential cross-over hit though I don't think SCC cares whether it crosses over or not. It's a song for fathers and daughters and how the years pass so quickly. Influence of the modern praise and worship music is manifest with the stirring anthems of Yours, Children of God, and With One Voice, all of which could easily be added to the repertoire of Passion Conferences.

This is the 21st SCC album and it just keeps getting better.
